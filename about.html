Mission


Sainik Aadhar Fund is a non-profit, non-governmental organization focused on providing financial support to membersof the Armed Forces (including Para Military Forces) and their dependents. In past 5 years , over 20000 families have received financial aid from us. Sainik Aadhar Fund was set up to take charge of voluntary donation in cash.
